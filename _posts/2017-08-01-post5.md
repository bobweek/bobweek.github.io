I had a lot of fun interacting with fellow scientists and presenting my research at the Evolution conference this year. Looking forward to next year! 

I presented an outline of the method I've been working on for inferring the strength of coevolution. At the time of this presentation, the method had only been worked out for mutualisms, however it has now been extended to antagonisms as well. My slides are available <a href="https://bobweek.github.io/files/Evolution Talk.pdf">here</a>.
