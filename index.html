Bob Week

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Idaho working with professor Scott Nuismer. My degree program is Bioinformatics and Computational Biology (BCB) which is housed within The Institute for Bioinformatics and Evolutionary Studies (IBEST).

My interests are in extending coevolutionary theory to the community context. Currently I am focused on doing this entirely in terms of phenotypic evolution, that is, deriving all of my models from a quantitative genetic (QG) foundation. Hence, I live in the world of microevolution. However, I have begun to think about larger spatial and temporal scales and thus how to marry QG with phylogenetics and metacommunity ecology.
