Bob Week

I am a student at the University of Idaho working on a PhD under Scott Nuismer. My degree program is Bioinformatics and Computational Biology (BCB) which is housed within the Institute for Evolutionary Studies (IBEST).

My research focuses on using quantitative genetic theory to investigate the evolutionary stability of biological communities. To do this I develop rigorous mathematical models of phenotypic evolution that incorporate species interactions and methods for inferring parameters of these models.
